Confused about Korean units?

  • scale
    1 Geun (근)
    ~600g (meat) or 400g (vegetables). Usually for bulk buying.
  • water_drop
    1 Cup (컵)
    In Korea, 1 cup is often 200ml (metric), not 240ml (US).
  • restaurant
    1 Spoon (큰술)
    Roughly 1 tablespoon (15ml). "Jageun-sul" (작은술) is a teaspoon.
