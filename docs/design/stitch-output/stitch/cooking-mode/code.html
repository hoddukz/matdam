Step 3

Add pork & kimchi

Add the sliced pork and kimchi into the pot. Sauté over medium-high heat until the pork is no longer pink and the kimchi softens.

grocery Ingredients Needed

  • 200g Pork belly
  • 1 cup Aged Kimchi
  • 1 tbsp Sesame Oil
lightbulb

Rendering the fat from the pork belly adds a rich flavor base for the stew.
