Essential Ingredients

The building blocks of Korean flavor. Learn what they are and where to buy them.

Gochujang

Korean Chili Paste

Fermented chili paste. Savory, sweet, and spicy. The soul of many stews.

Doenjang

Soybean Paste

Earthy, funky, and salty. Similar to Miso but deeper and more robust.

Gochugaru

Red Chili Flakes

Sun-dried chili flakes. Provides heat and vibrant red color without the paste texture.

Chamgireum

Toasted Sesame Oil

Nutty, aromatic finishing oil. Never used for high-heat frying, only for flavor.
